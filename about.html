Who am I?

I am a father and husband that loves to cook food with fire and prefers clean code over a clean desk. I'd describe myself as a LEGO building, code writing, barbecuing, Disney World enthusiast that loves entrepreneurship.

There's something about creating the future that excites me.

I am the CEO of Codeup, a career accelerator that trains software development to students with little to no prior programming experience in 18 weeks with a 90% job placement rate.
